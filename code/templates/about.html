About

In the modern day, when you need to extract insights from spoken content in this age of information overload. Our system is one that best in converting speech to text with good accuracy. Personalization - the product does transcription of speech but also knows which speaker is speaking along with advanced technologies ASR (Automatic Speech Recognition), Speaker diarization etc.

The transcribed text is then parsed by a Natural Language Processing (NLP) system, such as NLTK which generates concise summaries of the transcripts. By automating this process, it acts as a time-saver and boosts productivity, thereby easing the management of huge volumes of audio information for businesses, academics & media professionals. Built for fast processing and efficient access to information, our system represents an improvement in speech processing technology.
